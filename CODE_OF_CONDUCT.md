# Code of Conduct

## The Short Version

Be decent to each other. This is a small open source project - there's no room for harassment, discrimination, or general nastiness.

## Expected Behaviour

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, PRs, and discussions
- Assume good faith
- Critique ideas, not people
- Accept feedback gracefully

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Harassment or personal attacks of any kind
- Discriminatory language or behaviour
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behaviour
- Publishing others' private information without consent

## Reporting

If something happens that violates this, you can reach me at **camillajgd@gmail.com**. I'll take it seriously.

## Enforcement

Depending on the situation: a warning, removal from the discussion, or a ban from the project.

---

This is meant to be a welcoming project. If it ever doesn't feel that way, please let me know.
